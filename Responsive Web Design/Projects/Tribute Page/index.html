  • 1879 - Albert Einstein is born in Ulm, Germany, the first child of Hermann and Pauline Einstein.
  • 1888 - Einstein is accepted into the Lupitold Gymnasium.
  • 1895 - Einsten fails the entry exam for Zurich Polytechnic and joins a school in Switzerland.
  • 1900 - Einstein recieves his mathematics diploma and begins to work on his thesis.
  • 1905 - Einstein completes his paper on quantum theory.
  • 1909 - Einstein is appointed professor of theoretical physics at Zurich Polytechnic.
  • 1922 - Einstein wins the Nobel Prize for his work on quantum theory.
  • 1933 - Einstein moves to the United States after Nazi's seize power in Germany.
  • 1955 - Einstein dies in New Jersey. His body is cremated and his papers are turned over to the Hebrew University of Jerusalem.
